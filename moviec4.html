Kingdom

Genre: Action, Historical Drama

Release Date: April 19, 2019

Director: Shinsuke Sato

Cast: Kento Yamazaki, Ryo Yoshizawa, Masami Nagasawa

Storyline

*Kingdom* is an epic historical drama based on the popular manga of the same name. Set in the Warring States period of China, it follows the journey of an orphaned war orphan named Xin, who dreams of becoming the greatest general under the heavens. With courage, determination, and unlikely alliances, he rises from humble beginnings to take part in battles that will decide the fate of a nation.
