🎥 UPCOMING MOVIE LOKHA

🎬 Lokah – Chapter One: Chandra

🛡️ Overview
First female-led superhero film in Malayalam cinema, launching the ambitious Lokah cinematic universe.
Directed and written by Dominic Arun, and produced by Dulquer Salmaan under Wayfarer Films.

📅 Production Timeline
Pre-production began September 13, 2024.
Filming started on September 12, 2024, and wrapped up by January 30, 2025.

🎭 Cast & Crew
Stars: Kalyani Priyadarshan (first superhero role), Naslen K. Gafoor.
Cinematography: Nimish Ravi
Editing: Chaman Chakko
Music: Jakes Bejoy
Action Choreography: Yannick Ben

✨ Story & Theme
Follows Chandra, who discovers extraordinary powers and fights dark forces.
Blends myth, sci-fi, folklore, and dystopian tones.

🎥 Cinematic Impact
Launches Lokah superhero universe, inspired by Kerala mythology.

📅 Release & Distribution
Scheduled for Onam 2025 (around August 28, 2025) with the widest theater rollout.
Teaser released in late July 2025.
